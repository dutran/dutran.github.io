Bio

I am a research scientist at Facebook AI. I graduated with a Ph.D. in computer science from Dartmouth College and an M.S. in computer science from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Before coming to Dartmouth, I was a research staff at Nanyang Technological University. My research interests are computer vision, machine learning and computer graphics, with specific interests in video understanding, representation learning, and vision and language.
